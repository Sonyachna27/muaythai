Welcome to the Pride Muay Thai League tournament! Founded by World and European Muay Thai Champion Niko Kovalevich, we provide aspiring athletes a safe environment to grow their skills and be part of a strong Muay Thai community. Join us as a participant, coach, or spectator to celebrate the art of Muay Thai.

About Niko Kovalevich

Niko Kovalevich is a highly experienced Muay Thai fighter and trainer with a deep passion for the sport. With over 200 fights and decades of dedication, he has competed at the highest levels and now focuses on helping the next generation of athletes grow in a safe and competitive environment. His journey led him to create Pride Muay Thai League, offering fighters of all levels the opportunity to develop their skills.

  • 30+ years in Muay Thai
  • Former member of the Russian National Team (4 years)
  • World & European Champion (amateur)
  • Transitioned to a professional career
